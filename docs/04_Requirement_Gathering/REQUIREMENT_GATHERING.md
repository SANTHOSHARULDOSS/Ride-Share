# Requirement Gathering & Stakeholder Analysis

This document details the methodologies, surveys, interviews, and user personas used to outline the core features of the Ride Share MVP.

---

## 1. Stakeholder Definitions

1. **Drivers (Car Owners):** Commuters driving fixed daily routes who wish to offset fuel/maintenance costs.
2. **Passengers:** Daily commuters searching for comfortable, affordable alternatives to crowded public transport or single-passenger taxi services.
3. **Administrator:** System monitors who manage users, review active ride statistics, and maintain operational settings.

---
